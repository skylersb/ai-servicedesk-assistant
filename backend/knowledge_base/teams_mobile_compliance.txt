Title:
Fix Teams mobile access / device compliance issue

Problem:
User cannot access Teams or company data on mobile due to compliance policy.

Applies To:
iPhone, Android, Microsoft Teams, Intune-managed devices

Steps:
1. Install Microsoft Teams (if not installed)
2. Install Company Portal app from the App Store or Google Play
3. Open Company Portal
4. Sign in with your work account
5. Follow steps to register your device
6. Ensure:
   - Passcode is enabled
   - Device is not jailbroken/rooted
   - OS is up to date
7. Reopen Teams after compliance is complete

Troubleshooting:
- Device not compliant → Complete all Company Portal steps
- Access denied → Wait a few minutes after setup
- Still blocked → Remove and re-add account

Escalation:
If your device shows compliant but still cannot access Teams, contact IT.
