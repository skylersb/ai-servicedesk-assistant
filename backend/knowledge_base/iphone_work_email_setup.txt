Title:
Set up work email on iPhone

Problem:
User wants to add their work email account to the built-in Mail app on an iPhone.

Applies To:
iPhone, iOS Mail app, Microsoft 365, Exchange Online

Steps:
1. Open Settings on your iPhone
2. Scroll down and tap Mail
3. Tap Accounts
4. Tap Add Account
5. Select Microsoft Exchange
6. Enter your work email address
7. Tap Next
8. Sign in with your work password
9. Complete any multi-factor authentication if prompted
10. Choose which items to sync (Mail, Contacts, Calendars)
11. Tap Save

Troubleshooting:
- Sign-in page does not load → Check internet connection and try again
- Password rejected → Confirm your Microsoft 365 password works at portal.office.com
- MFA prompt not received → Open Microsoft Authenticator and approve manually
- Mail not syncing after setup → Remove the account and add it again

Escalation:
If you cannot complete sign-in or the account will not sync after re-adding it, contact IT support.
