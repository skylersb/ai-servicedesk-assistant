Title:
Reset your work password

Problem:
User cannot sign in due to forgotten or expired password.

Applies To:
All users, Microsoft 365, Entra ID

Steps:
1. Go to: https://passwordreset.microsoftonline.com
2. Enter your work email
3. Complete verification (phone, email, or Authenticator)
4. Create a new password

Password requirements:
- At least 8 characters
- Uppercase + lowercase
- Number or symbol

Troubleshooting:
- Didn’t receive code → Check spam or try another method
- Authenticator not working → Use phone/email option
- Account locked → Wait 15 minutes and retry

Escalation:
If you cannot verify your identity, contact IT for a manual reset.
