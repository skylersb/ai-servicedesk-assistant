Title:
Set up Microsoft Authenticator on a new phone

Problem:
User is not receiving MFA push notifications after switching to a new phone.

Applies To:
iPhone, Android, Microsoft Authenticator, Entra ID (Azure AD)

Steps:
1. Install Microsoft Authenticator from the App Store or Google Play
2. Open the app and tap "Add account"
3. Select "Work or school account"
4. On a computer, go to: https://aka.ms/mfasetup
5. Sign in with your work account
6. Click "Add sign-in method"
7. Choose "Authenticator app"
8. Scan the QR code with your phone
9. Approve the test notification
10. Remove your old device from the Security Info page (if listed)

Troubleshooting:
- No push notifications → Enable notifications for Authenticator in phone settings
- QR code not working → Retry setup or choose manual entry
- App not prompting → Remove and re-add the account
- Still failing → Restart phone and try again

Escalation:
If you cannot sign in or lost access to your old device, contact IT to reset MFA.
