If Outlook keeps asking for your password, it usually means your saved sign-in details are out of sync.

Try these steps:
1. Close Outlook completely
2. Reopen Outlook and sign in again when prompted
3. If it keeps happening, restart your computer
4. Make sure you recently approved any sign-in request on your phone (if using MFA)

If the issue continues, IT may need to reset your account or clear saved credentials.
